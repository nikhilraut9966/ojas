Why choose Health Insurance?

Health insurance protects you and your family from unexpected medical expenses. It ensures access to quality treatment without financial stress, covering hospitalisation, surgeries, daycare procedures, and more.

Key benefits

  • Cashless treatment across network hospitals
  • Coverage for hospitalisation & major surgeries
  • Daycare procedures & pre/post-hospitalisation
  • No-claim bonus & wellness benefits
  • Optional riders: critical illness, personal accident cover
